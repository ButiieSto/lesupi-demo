About the Book

Welcome to Hypercondria™ — a flawless, medically optimized town where not having a disorder is the ultimate red flag.

Here, wellness is currency, therapy is mandatory, and your daily dose of medication isn’t just encouraged — it’s celebrated. From Diagnosipops™ to Sadness Management Seminars, everyone’s a proud patient. Feeling fine? You’re clearly in denial.

Enter Hloux, a quiet skeptic with a suspiciously clean medical record. When a chance encounter with a rebellious soul named Noni exposes him to the glitch beneath the system’s polished smile, Hloux begins to see the truth: they don’t need to drug you — just convince you to medicate yourself.

As they descend deeper into the pharmaceutical funhouse, dodging Side-Effect Evangelists, Dopamine Influencers, and pill-pushing prophets, Hloux and Noni must unlearn everything the town has branded “self-care.” Because beneath the synthetic joy and curated neuroses, something unmedicated is waking up...

And it’s not approved by the Council of Diagnostics™.
