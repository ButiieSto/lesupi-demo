Welcome to:

Lithako Edu & Sports

Empowering our young minds through education & sports.

Our structured training programs are tailored for learners aged 5 to 15, combining academic support with high-performance sports development across Football, Basketball, Netball, Athletics, and more.

With qualified coaches, safe facilities, and results-driven programs, we empower every child to reach their full potential, academically, physically, and mentally. We’re here to nurture their confidence, teach teamwork, and show them that dedication pays off both in the classroom and on the field.

Through our balanced approach to education and sport, children learn discipline, respect, and resilience, which are values that will carry them far beyond the now. Developing complete athletes, because every child deserves a chance.
