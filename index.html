About Lesupi

Lesupi Ranneile is a dedicated educator, author, and mentor passionate about empowering young learners and the next generation of educators. Based in Matlosana City, he runs Lithako Edu and Sports to support learning in practical, engaging ways.

He believes in the power of books, education, and mentorship to unlock potential and inspire greatness in youth and tutors alike.
